About Us

The Cornell Association of Officiating Students (C.A.O.S.) is a student run organization dedicated to providing organizations with quality officials by promoting better officiating through various student development opportunities, including but not limited to clinics, tournaments, meetings and connections with local and national officiating bodies. These extramural opportunities will not only promote better officiating through increasing overall knowledge of respective sports but also provide transferable job-related skills and encourage cooperative attitudes and working relationships.
