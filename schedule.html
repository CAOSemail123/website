Schedule

Meetings

Meetings will be held at the Noyes Recreational Center Conference Room. During each meeting, we will go over officiating games, get to know each other better, and discuss opportunities to get more involved.

Working at the Ithaca Youth Bureau

CAOS has consistently engaged with the local community through sports. Currently, we partner with the Ithaca Youth Bureau. We provide officials for their recreational sports, have served as sports coaches for kids from K-12, and raised money through sports tournaments. We have long-lasting relationship and joining CAOS can give you an opportunity to give back to the local community!
